How to Play

WikiGuessr

Guess which Wikipedia article received more views over the last 30 days.

  • Easy: Top 1000 most viewed articles.
  • Medium: Random articles, full page visible.
  • Hard: Random articles, only title and snippet visible.

WikiSpeedrun

Navigate from a starting article to a target article using only the links inside the pages.

  • Easy: Counts time/clicks. Can undo moves.
  • Hard: 5 minute limit. No going back.
