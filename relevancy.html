How to Play

Welcome to Relevancy.

The goal is simple: guess which Wikipedia article received more views over the last 30 days.

  • Easy: Top 1000 most viewed articles. High recognizability.
  • Medium: Completely random articles. Full page viewable.
  • Hard: Completely random articles. Only the title and a short snippet are provided.

Score as high as you can before making a mistake! All credit goes to the editors and authors for Wikipedia.
